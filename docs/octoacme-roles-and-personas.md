# OctoAcme Personas

This document defines typical roles and responsibilities used in OctoAcme project docs and exercises.

---

## Developers

### Role Summary
Developers design, build, test, and deliver software components. They collaborate with product and project leads to implement features that meet acceptance criteria and quality standards.

### Responsibilities
- Implement features and fixes to meet acceptance criteria
- Write and maintain tests and documentation
- Participate in design and code reviews
- Assist in estimating and planning work
- Help identify technical risks and propose mitigations

### Goals
- Deliver reliable, maintainable code
- Reduce cycle time from idea to production
- Maintain high test coverage and observability

### Typical Communication
- Daily standups and sprint planning
- PR descriptions and code review comments
- Technical design docs when needed

---

## Product Managers

### Role Summary
Product Managers define what should be built to deliver customer and business value. They own the product vision, prioritize the backlog, and measure outcomes.

### Responsibilities
- Define problem statements and success metrics
- Prioritize the roadmap and backlog
- Collaborate with stakeholders and engineering on trade-offs
- Validate solutions through user research and metrics

### Goals
- Maximize customer value and impact
- Make clear, data-driven prioritization decisions
- Ensure product-market fit and usability

### Typical Communication
- Weekly alignment with PM and engineering leads
- Roadmap updates and stakeholder briefings
- Acceptance criteria and feature specs

---

## Project Managers

### Role Summary
Project Managers coordinate delivery activities, manage schedules, risks, and communications. They enable the team to deliver on commitments efficiently.

### Responsibilities
- Create and maintain project plans and timelines
- Manage risks, dependencies, and resource constraints
- Facilitate meetings (kickoff, planning, retrospectives)
- Ensure consistent project documentation and status reporting
- Coordinate cross-team and stakeholder communication

### Goals
- Deliver projects on time and within scope
- Minimize unplanned work and escalations
- Maintain transparency and alignment across stakeholders

### Typical Communication
- Weekly status updates and stakeholder reports
- Risk registers and decision logs
- Coordination via project boards and meeting facilitation

---

## Scrum Master

### Role Summary
Scrum Masters facilitate agile ceremonies, remove blockers, and ensure the team follows agreed processes. They coach the team on agile practices and foster continuous improvement.

### Responsibilities
- Facilitate sprint planning, daily standups, retrospectives, and reviews
- Remove impediments and blockers that prevent team progress
- Coach team members on agile practices and self-organization
- Shield the team from external interruptions and distractions
- Track team metrics and help identify process improvements

### Goals
- Maximize team velocity and predictability
- Foster a collaborative and transparent team culture
- Continuously improve team processes and practices

### Typical Communication
- Daily standups and sprint ceremonies facilitation
- Weekly sync with Project Manager on blockers and dependencies
- Retrospective action items and team feedback
- Escalation of systemic issues to leadership

### Key Collaboration Points
- Works closely with Project Manager on delivery coordination
- Partners with Developers to identify and resolve blockers
- Collaborates with Product Manager on backlog refinement
- Interfaces with QA Lead on testing workflows and quality gates

---

## QA Lead

### Role Summary
QA Leads oversee test strategy, quality gates, and acceptance validation. They ensure features meet quality standards before release and work closely with developers and project managers.

### Responsibilities
- Define and maintain test strategy and quality standards
- Review acceptance criteria and testability of features
- Coordinate testing activities across manual and automated tests
- Manage quality gates and sign-off before releases
- Track quality metrics and defect trends

### Goals
- Ensure high-quality, reliable releases
- Minimize production defects and regressions
- Maintain comprehensive test coverage

### Typical Communication
- Test plans and quality reports
- Defect triage and prioritization meetings
- Sign-off approvals for releases
- Quality metrics dashboards

### Key Collaboration Points
- Partners with Developers on test automation and testability
- Works with Project Manager on release readiness and timelines
- Collaborates with Product Manager on acceptance criteria
- Interfaces with DevOps Engineer on CI/CD test integration
- Coordinates with Technical Writer on testing documentation

---

## UX Designer

### Role Summary
UX Designers shape user flows, create prototypes, and drive usability improvements. They advocate for the end user and ensure products are intuitive and delightful.

### Responsibilities
- Conduct user research and usability testing
- Create wireframes, prototypes, and design specifications
- Define user flows and interaction patterns
- Collaborate on design systems and component libraries
- Validate designs through user feedback and metrics

### Goals
- Deliver intuitive, user-centered experiences
- Improve user satisfaction and engagement metrics
- Ensure consistency across product interfaces

### Typical Communication
- Design critiques and prototype reviews
- User research findings and personas
- Design specifications and handoff documentation
- Usability testing reports

### Key Collaboration Points
- Partners with Product Manager on user needs and feature definition
- Works closely with Developers on implementation feasibility
- Collaborates with QA Lead on usability testing
- Interfaces with Technical Writer on user-facing documentation
- Engages with Stakeholders on design direction

---

## Technical Writer

### Role Summary
Technical Writers author and maintain documentation for internal teams and external users. They ensure clear, accurate, and accessible documentation throughout the project lifecycle.

### Responsibilities
- Write and maintain user guides, API docs, and internal process documentation
- Review technical accuracy with subject matter experts
- Maintain documentation standards and style guides
- Update docs based on feature changes and user feedback
- Organize and structure documentation for discoverability

### Goals
- Provide clear, accurate documentation
- Reduce support burden through self-service resources
- Maintain documentation consistency and quality

### Typical Communication
- Documentation reviews with developers and product teams
- Style guide updates and standards discussions
- Doc release notes and update announcements
- Feedback loops with support and user communities

### Key Collaboration Points
- Works with Developers on technical accuracy and code examples
- Partners with Product Manager on feature documentation priorities
- Collaborates with UX Designer on user-facing content
- Interfaces with QA Lead on testing documentation
- Coordinates with Project Manager on documentation timelines

---

## DevOps Engineer

### Role Summary
DevOps Engineers build and maintain CI/CD pipelines, infrastructure, and deployment automation. They enable fast, reliable releases and support the team's delivery workflow.

### Responsibilities
- Design and maintain CI/CD pipelines and automation
- Manage infrastructure, monitoring, and alerting
- Implement security scanning and compliance checks
- Support deployment processes and incident response
- Optimize build times and deployment reliability

### Goals
- Enable fast, safe, automated deployments
- Maintain high system reliability and uptime
- Reduce manual toil through automation

### Typical Communication
- Pipeline status and build reports
- Infrastructure change notifications
- Incident response and post-mortems
- Performance and reliability metrics

### Key Collaboration Points
- Partners with Developers on build and deployment processes
- Works with QA Lead on test automation infrastructure
- Collaborates with Project Manager on release scheduling
- Interfaces with Scrum Master on velocity and flow metrics
- Supports Technical Writer with infrastructure documentation

---

## Stakeholder

### Role Summary
Stakeholders provide strategic guidance, business requirements, and represent key business units or customer segments. They provide input on priorities and approve major decisions.

### Responsibilities
- Provide business context and strategic direction
- Review and approve major deliverables and milestones
- Communicate business requirements and constraints
- Participate in key reviews and decision gates
- Champion the project within their organization

### Goals
- Ensure alignment with business objectives
- Maximize return on investment
- Represent constituent needs in project decisions

### Typical Communication
- Monthly status updates and milestone reviews
- Business requirement discussions
- Decision gate approvals
- Strategic alignment meetings

### Key Collaboration Points
- Provides input to Product Manager on business priorities
- Reviews progress with Project Manager at key milestones
- Approves major decisions and scope changes with Sponsor
- Engages with UX Designer on user experience validation

---

## Sponsor

### Role Summary
Sponsors own project funding, provide executive support, and make final escalation decisions. They ensure the project has necessary resources and organizational backing.

### Responsibilities
- Secure and allocate project budget and resources
- Provide executive sponsorship and organizational support
- Resolve escalated issues and remove organizational blockers
- Approve major scope, timeline, or resource changes
- Champion project value at leadership level

### Goals
- Ensure project delivers expected business value
- Remove organizational barriers to success
- Maintain executive alignment and support

### Typical Communication
- Quarterly business reviews
- Escalation decisions and resolutions
- Budget and resource approvals
- Executive status briefings

### Key Collaboration Points
- Partners with Project Manager on resource allocation and escalations
- Works with Product Manager on strategic alignment
- Receives updates from Stakeholders on business impact
- Makes final decisions on scope and priority trade-offs
